I am a BEng student in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Nottingham, UK. I am part of the 2+2 articulation programme, having completed my first two years at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China before transferring to the UK campus.

My recent work spans robotics, reinforcement learning, computer vision, and LLMs, including research experience at Tsinghua University, Xi'an Jiaotong University, the Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data, and the University of Science and Technology of China.
